Topic 1.1: Scope of Practice and Roles of Members of Interprofessional Teams

Scope of Practice and Roles of Members of Interprofessional Teams. Welcome to this topic on the Scope of Practice and Roles of Members of Interprofessional Teams. This topic will focus on the scope of practice of members of interprofessional teams. You will also examine the roles of members of interprofessional teams.

Learning Targets

At the conclusion of this topic, you will be able to:
  • Explain the scope of practice of members of interprofessional teams
  • Describe the roles of members of interprofessional teams
